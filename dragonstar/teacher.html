教 师 信 息

Prof. Hong Zhang received her Ph.D in computational mathematics from Michigan State University in 1989. She was a math professor at Clemson University of South Carolina and Louisiana State University until she joined the PETSc development team at Argonne National Laboratory (ANL) USA, in 1999. Since then, she has been teaching graduate courses on advanced scientific computing, software design and data structures in the Department of Computer Science at Illinois Institute of Technology as a research professor, and worked as a core developer in the PETSc team. At ANL, she conducts large scale scientific and engineering simulations on advanced computer systems, which includes cutting-edge numerical software development and various emerging applications.
